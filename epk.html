BIOGRAPHY

Benjamín Londoño, known as djbenjax, is a 13-year-old Colombian DJ and producer with a deep passion for electronic music. From a very young age, he showed interest in music, starting with guitar, drums, and piano, and from the age of 7, he became fascinated with the world of EDM. Trained at DNA Music, Tomorrowland Academy, and MadSkills, he has developed his own style within genres such as Tech House, Afro House, UK Garage, and Drum & Bass.

Throughout his career, Benjamín has participated in private events and unique presentations, such as the EVA fair or Beat_Scene, combining his technical skills with unique creativity that transforms each set into an immersive experience. Additionally, his talent extends to music production, graphic design, 3D modeling, and video editing, allowing him to create a comprehensive and complete artistic project.

With a constant focus on growing as an artist and connecting with his audience, Benjax seeks to bring the energy of his sets to every stage, generating memorable experiences both live and through digital platforms.
